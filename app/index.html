Love, True Love

Melinda + Craig

The couple first met at a mutual friend's party in October 2011, where Melinda willingly exchanged details with Craig, but only after discovering his current flat mate was an IT recruiter, as she was looking for a change.

Craig promptly started chatting via facebook and email with her, and a month down the line Melinda finally cottoned on that Craig liked her.

Their first date was making firework cupcakes in Melinda's kitchen for a Guy Fawkes night party. From that day onwards they were virtually inseparable.

In January 2012 they embarked on their first of many ski trips together as part of the ski group her sister organises each year. They had such a wonderful time and made it through the whole week in total serenity, which gave Melinda complete confidence that they were ready to live with each other. Just a few months later, in June 2012, they moved in together.

A year and a half later in December 2013, to Melinda's great surprise, Craig got down on one knee and proposed to his beloved Melinda. Naturally she said yes.
