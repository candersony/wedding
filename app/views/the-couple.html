The Bride

Melinda Bramwell-King

Melinda was born and raised in the quiet city of Perth in Western Australia.

As a child, always hoping for adventure and excitement, Melinda dreamed of travelling the world. After University, she decided to seek her fortune far from home, exploring Europe and eventually settling in England.

Over the years, Melinda has travelled widely throughout the world while also establishing herself well as a Senior Data Analyst in London. She continues to persue her passion for Historical Re-enacting, Costume Design, Karaoke, Skiing and Scuba Diving.

Love, True Love

Melinda & Craig

The couple first met at a mutual friend's party in October 2011. Melinda willingly exchanged details with Craig, but only after discovering his current flat mate was an IT recruiter and she was looking for a change.

Craig promptly started chatting via facebook and email, and a month down the line Melinda finally cottoned on that Craig liked her.

Their first date was making firework cupcakes in Melinda's kitchen for a Guy Fawkes night party. From that day onwards they were virtually inseparable.

In January 2012, they embarked on their first of many ski trips together. They had such a wonderful time and made it through the whole week in total serenity, which gave Melinda complete confidence that they were ready to live with each other. Just a few months later, in June 2012, they moved in together.

A year and a half later in December 2013, to Melinda's great surprise, Craig got down on one knee and proposed to his beloved. Naturally she said yes.
